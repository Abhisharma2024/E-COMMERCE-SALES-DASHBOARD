# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

> **An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, payment trends, and operational KPIs through dynamic visualizations and data-driven insights.**

---

# 📖 Project Overview

The **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed in **Microsoft Power BI**. It enables businesses to monitor key sales metrics, customer purchasing behavior, product category performance, payment preferences, and shipping insights through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard converts raw sales data into meaningful insights that help stakeholders make informed business decisions, identify sales trends, improve operational efficiency, and enhance customer experience.

- Track sales growth over time

# 📐 DAX Measures

```DAX
Total Revenue =
SUM(Orders[Revenue])

Total Orders =
COUNT(Orders[OrderID])

Total Customers =
DISTINCTCOUNT(Orders[CustomerID])

Average Order Value =
DIVIDE([Total Revenue],[Total Orders])

Average Rating =
AVERAGE(Orders[ReviewRating])

Cancellation % =
DIVIDE(
CALCULATE(
COUNT(Orders[OrderID]),
Orders[OrderStatus]="Cancelled"
),
COUNT(Orders[OrderID])
)

Return % =
DIVIDE(
CALCULATE(
COUNT(Orders[OrderID]),
Orders[OrderStatus]="Returned"
),
COUNT(Orders[OrderID])
)
```

# 💼 Business Insights

### Revenue Performance

- Generated over **31.55 Million** in total revenue.
- Consistent sales observed across multiple product categories.

### Customer Analysis

- More than **12,000** unique customers.
- Average customer rating of **2.99**.

- Digital payment methods were the most preferred by customers.
- Cash on Delivery remained a significant payment option.

- USA recorded the highest average order value.
- Major metropolitan cities generated consistent revenue.
